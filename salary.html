Description

There are many different wages for law related careers, and salary depends on which level of law one works in.

Federal Government level jobs make around $145,160 a year.

Legal Services level jobs make around $122.150 a year.

Local Government level jobs make around $94,490 a year.

State Government level jobs make around $86,900 a year.
